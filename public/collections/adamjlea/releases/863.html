Notes
All titles composed by U2.

Tracks 1, 4: Location recording by Steve Lillywhite. Produced for Source by EDR Entertainment and recorded on the Effanel Music Mobile Facility, Denver, Co. June 5th, 1983.

Track 2: Location recording, produced by DIR Broadcasting and recorded on the Effanel Music Mobile, Boston, Mass. May 6th, 1983.

Tracks 3, 5, 6, 7, 8: Location recording courtesy of "Rockpalast 83", WDR Television, West Germany, August 20th, 1983.

During the performance of "The Electric Co.", Bono included a 27-second snippet of Stephen Sondheim's "Send in the Clowns". This gave licensing issues in some countries. This release does not include this snippet and has a shorter actual duration (4:51 instead of 5:18) which is also on the label.

The actual duration of track B1 is 5:25. Label wrongly shows 4:55 however actual duration listed after song title.
