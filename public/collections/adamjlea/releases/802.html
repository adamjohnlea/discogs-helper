1978 Japanese-edition vinyl album + OBI with rainbow labels. This release includes a gatefold lyric insert in English and Japanese.

Initial copies included a card enabling the purchaser to request a copy of [r=1846628] in-store. The flexi was not supplied by default with the LP.
