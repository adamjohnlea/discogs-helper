Notes
Capitol, Los Angeles pressing.

This is the original 1979 pressing with yellow "B-52's" labels. There is no barcode. The other pressings with barcode and alternate Warner Brothers labels can be found at [r3471539], [r3471674] and [r3476496].

Also known with a silver "Promotional Not For Sale" stamp on the front cover.

Recorded at Compass Point Studios, Nassau, Bahamas.

Issued with custom inner sleeve with lyrics and credits.
